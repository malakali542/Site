Malak Ali

Hi there! I'm a first year Systems Design Engineering student @ the University of Waterloo.

I have previously worked as a Product Designer at M.A Kharafi & Sons building a website to highlight their recent construction project, as a Mechatronics Designer @ Top Hat Robotics to design and develop tech, and as a Full Stack Developer @ MAX Gala , designing "MAX Aspire", a new platform to connect the MAX community.

Besides being obsessed with Design and Software, I've been interested in Biotechnology and am now a marketing executive for BioTEC 2020, Canada's premier biotechnology conference! After being introduced AI & Machine learning at several hackathons, I am currently earning a certificate in Machine Learning from Stanford University.

A designer at heart, I aim to view problems from a "systems thinking" viewpoint and find efficient ways to solve them with design. After all, systems is how we view the world, and design is how we change it.

When I'm not designing or developing, you can find me drawing, watching NBA games + pre/post game shows, playing frisbee, or geeking out about the latest Survivor elimination.

Feel free to reach out for coffee(I prefer tea) or just to chat!
