MAX Aspire Mockups

As a part of the MAX Aspire design and development team, I am playing a role in creating mockups, participating in design reviews, and reiterating designs on a team of three designers.

My Role : User Interface Design

Overview : Below you will be able to follow the design process of out present day Landing Page and Dashboard mockups that are still being improved on! You'll be able to see the process from simple sketches to high-fidelity mockups.

Initial Sketches.

I created some rough sketches based on MAX's main landing page and tried to be consistent with their current branding.

First Round of Mockups - Landing Page & Dashboard

These prototypes were created under a time constraint of 48 hours before our next meeting. Looking back at these initial wireframes, I definitely was at the start of my experience with Figma and had not become proficient with it yet.

Current Round of Mockups - Landing Page

Our current round of Landing Page mockups is a product of collaboration between the designers and we are still looking forward to add further changes. Of which include identifying in what manner we want some our information to be displayed, for example, we are looking at carousels, sliders, static jumbotrons etc.

Current Round of Mockups - Dashboard

Our Dashboard has also changed thorugh design reiterations. Though we have a basic structure of our Dashboard, a lot of work is still going into the smaller UI components. Our main focus is making the different pages such as jobs, coffee chats, events, and home have their own unique UI elements. For example, we are working on incorporating a distinct coffee icon to customize the coffee chats page and add a layer of visual interest.

I'm excited to see how our final mockups will turn out. To chat about the current designs feel free to email me!
